School Library

Our School was founded in 1 June 1886 (Including the establish of Library)

The DAV University Library is also known as Knowledge Resources Center, is on the path of continuous development and expansion since 2013 and fulfing the information need of its users . It is located at a central place of university campus. Library is under CCTV surveillance system with a view to put keen eye to variety of user library. Library is air- conditioned furnished with modular and comfortable furniture having sitting capacity of more than 250 loaded with LED lights . The Library subscribes more than 100 national/international journals, magazines and newspapers in print . School Canteen

Our School was founded in 1 June 1886 (Including the establish of Canteen)

The College Canteen is the essential part of the colleges and a popular place both with students and staff. It is located on the ground floor and easily accessible to all. The canteen is well equipped with furniture, hall and a cabin to cater to the staff of the college. There is a variety of food items served in a clean, fresh condition and at reasonable rates. It is perhaps the busiest place in the college where every student is expected to pay at least one or may be two visits a day for quick snacks, cold drinks, tea, some gossip or a discussion on courses taught, youth festival, talent show etc.
